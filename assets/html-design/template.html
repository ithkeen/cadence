Overview

Stencil & Tablet treats type as the layout. The bone field carries everything; saturated tablet cards punch through to mark every callout, statistic, and pull quote. In the scrolling doc form the scale comes down but the voice stays.

Body text runs in Inter at 18 / 1.7. Headlines run in Stardos Stencil 700 uppercase. Chrome runs in Barlow Condensed extra-heavy with generous tracking. Body is the only sentence-case voice in the system.

Principles

TL;DR

Four rules that earn this system its identity.

  • Scale leads — numerals and headlines run as big as the column allows.
  • Color is layout — saturated tablet blocks divide the page.
  • Flat & rounded — no shadow, no gradient, 22–26px corners only.
  • Stencil voice — every headline in Stardos Stencil uppercase.
01

Scale leads

Size is the primary expressive tool. Even at doc scale, the hero runs at 112px.

02

Color is layout

Saturated tablets divide the bone field. The blocks themselves carry the structure.

03

Flat & rounded

No drop shadow. No gradient. Depth = saturation + 22–26px corner radius. That's the entire system.

04

Stencil voice

Stardos Stencil at every scale. The ink-break gaps are the system's identity.
